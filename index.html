  • MONTAG

    • MONTAG.WTF

      MONTAG Magazine was a futurist magazine sponsored by the marketing department of Grover from March 2017 – April 2019. It had a pretty cool website that is now only available through the Internet Archive Wayback Machine. There were three print issues: TOGETHER TOMORROW, BETTER BODIES, and CODING CREATIVITY.

    • MONTAG.XYZ

      When MONTAG the magazine ended in early 2019, I was beginning to be interested in LLMs and text generation (before it was cool) and created MONTAG.XYZ as its immortal AI-powered spawn and published it from May 2019 - June 2020. I wrote up the project including how I did the text scraping to create the data set and how I used Max Woolf's gpt-2-simple and Spell to train and sample from the model in Towards Data Science, "The magazine that writes itself" in June of 2019. Unfortunately Spell, the ML ops platform I wrote this guide for, is now defunct after being bought by Reddit in 2022.

  • Designing Embodied Conversational Interface Agents

    Thesis written in 2018 for my master's degree in Communications Design from HMKW Berlin. I wrote about human-computer interaction and natural language interfaces, and proposed a framework for designing interfaces which considers embodiment, anthropomorphism, realism, and situation as guiding principles. It also features case studies of well-known agents such as Clippy, Mavis Beacon, Ask Jeeves, and Realbotix Harmony AI. read it here

  • Cyber Corpus

    Thesis written in 2014 for my bachelor's degree in Art from Reed College. I wrote about embodiment and representation of the female body in cyberfeminist net art of the early 2000s, alternative (disembodied, fragmented, and reincarnated) embodiments, and embodiment alternatives in the bardo of cyberspace. read it here
